Our Mentors

Meet the STEM professionals and educators who guide NextGen STEM Leaders. They help students connect classroom learning to real-world careers.

Dr. Maya Johnson

Biomedical Engineer
Health & Science Pathways

Dr. Johnson mentors students who are curious about medicine, biology, and public health. She leads virtual lab walk-throughs and helps students explore college majors related to STEM and healthcare.

Alex Rivera

Software Developer
Coding & App Design

Alex supports students who want to learn to code, build apps, and understand how software powers the tools they use every day. They introduce basic programming concepts and share project ideas students can try at home or in class.

Jordan Lee

Civil Engineer
Design & Infrastructure

Jordan helps students see how math and science shape the world around them. They discuss bridges, transportation systems, and sustainable design, and encourage students to sketch their own ideas.
