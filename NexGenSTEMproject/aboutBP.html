About NextGen STEM Leaders

NextGen STEM Leaders is dedicated to empowering students from elementary to high school to explore their curiosity and develop confidence in STEM fields.

Since our founding, we have focused on providing accessible resources, supportive mentors, and engaging activities that encourage students to think creatively and work collaboratively.

Our team includes educators, industry professionals, and passionate volunteers who believe in shaping the next generation of problem solvers and innovators.
